[User Enters Room ID & UserID]
        |
        ↓
[roomidinput.tsx]
    ⇨ Sends "join" msg to server
        |
        ↓
[Server (index.ts)]
    ⇨ Stores socket + RoomID + UserID
        |
        ↓
[User Types Message]
        |
        ↓
[inputmessage.tsx]
    ⇨ Sends "chat" msg to server
        |
        ↓
[Server (index.ts)]
    ⇨ Broadcasts msg to others in room
        |
        ↓
[App.tsx]
    ⇨ Receives msg via ws.onmessage
        |
        ↓
    ⇨ Adds to `messages[]`
        |
        ↓
    ⇨ Renders user + message on screen
